All of a sudden there is a loud bang from one of the metal lab doors. A
sizeable dent has been made, and now a second, and now a third. The banging is
relentless, you turn to run but it is too late. The metal door bursts off it's
hinges to reveal a man who seems normal up until you look at his head, which
has mushrooms growing out of his skull. The only way out now is to fight.
